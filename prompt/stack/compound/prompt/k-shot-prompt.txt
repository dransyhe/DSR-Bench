Q: What is the final stack, when performing the following operations:
(push, 12)
(push, 35)
(pop)
(pop)
(push, 45)
A: The final stack is [45].

Q: What is the final stack, when performing the following operations:
(push, 65)
(push, 0)
(pop)
(push, 92)
(push, 12)
A: The final stack is [65, 92, 12].
