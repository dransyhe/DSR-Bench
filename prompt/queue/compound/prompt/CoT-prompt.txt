Q: What is the final queue, when performing the following operations:
(enqueue, 21)
(enqueue, 3)
(dequeue)
(dequeue)
(enqueue, 48)
A: Initially, the queue is [].
After (enqueue, 21), it becomes [21].
After (enqueue, 3), it becomes [21, 3].
After (dequeue), it becomes [3].
After (dequeue), it becomes [].
After (enqueue, 48), it becomes [48].
The final queue is [48].
